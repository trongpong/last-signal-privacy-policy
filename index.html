Privacy Policy

Last Signal
Developed by TrongPong
Last updated: March 23, 2026

Overview

Last Signal is a tower defense game. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how data is handled. This policy explains what information is collected when you use the app.

Information We Collect

Last Signal itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. All game progress (settings, campaign progress, scores, and statistics) is stored locally on your device and is never sent over the network.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services, which may collect data as described in their own privacy policies:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display optional rewarded video ads. AdMob may collect and process data including:

  • Device identifiers (advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location (based on IP)
  • App usage and interaction data
  • Device information (model, OS version)

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Google Play Billing

In-app purchases are processed through Google Play Billing. Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Google Play. We do not collect or store any payment information. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Children's Privacy

Last Signal is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. AdMob is not configured to serve personalized ads to children.

Data Storage

Game save data is stored locally on your device in app-private storage. This data is not accessible to other apps and is deleted when you uninstall the game. We do not operate any servers that receive your data.

Permissions

The app requests the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET — Required to load ads and process in-app purchases.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — Required to check network availability before loading ads.

Your Choices

  • Ads: You can purchase the "Remove Ads" option to disable all advertising.
  • Ad personalization: You can opt out of personalized ads through your device's settings (Settings > Google > Ads).
  • Data deletion: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
